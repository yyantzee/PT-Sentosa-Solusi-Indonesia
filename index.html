ABOUT US

WHO

WE

ARE

Sentosa Solutions was established in 2017. formerly GLM Sistem Indonesia. In 2022 we restructured with new strategy and established Sentosa Solusi Indonesia. We are a System Integrator and provider of Digital Solutions, Outsourcing, and Shared Services for companies in Indonesia.

Actively contribute to the IT field in innovation and development of information system solutions. Sharing experience expertise with other businesses and industries in a mutually beneficial scheme. We also contribute to communities and countries to increase the adoption of digitalization to grow competitiveness and make life easier.
